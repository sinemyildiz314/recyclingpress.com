CHINAPLAS 2025: Witness the Future of Plastics Circularity

The Plastics Circularity Journey: A Live Demo

The plastics recycling industry is experiencing a surge in innovation and growth. CHINAPLAS 2025 will spotlight this vital trend with "The Plastics Circularity Journey" Production Line Live Demo. This immersive experience will showcase the high-value utilization and cutting-edge technologies driving plastics recycling, emphasizing the principles of a circular economy.

Two distinct production lines will take center stage, demonstrating "Closed-Loop Bottle-to-Bottle Recycling" and "Turning Waste into Treasure," offering attendees a comprehensive view of the entire plastic recycling process.

Circularity: PET Food-Grade Recycling

Witness the journey of PET from waste to valuable resource! This live demonstration will encompass six key processes: material sorting, extrusion granulation, metal detection, underwater pelletizing, solid-state polymerization, and injection-blow molding. This comprehensive showcase of PET same-grade recycling, a first for CHINAPLAS, is made possible through the collaboration of industry leaders like Meyer Optoelectronics, Aceretech, Sesotec, Polycut, and BPET.

Turning Waste into Treasure: PE High-Quality Recycling

Discover the potential of PE plastics regeneration! This second live demonstration focuses on transforming post-consumer household bottles. The process, from crushing, washing, drying, and sorting to re-pelletizing, highlights the complete circular economy value of PE. This demonstration is a joint effort by Genox, Lauffer, and Aceretech.

Join Us at CHINAPLAS 2025

"The Plastics Circularity Journey" Production Line Live Demo is a landmark event for the global plastics industry, showcasing the latest advancements and breakthroughs in plastics recycling technology. Visit us at Booth 6J41 in Hall 6 during CHINAPLAS 2025. We invite you to witness firsthand the exciting future of the plastic circular economy!
